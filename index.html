About the Photographer

Finding wonder in wings, light, and fleeting moments.

After 45 years in retail management, retirement gave me the gift of time—and the chance to fully dive into photography, my lifelong passion. While I've always enjoyed landscapes, macro, and wildlife, everything changed three years ago when I went to a local park to photograph Bald Eagles, my spark bird. That single morning opened my eyes to the incredible variety of birds all around me. Since then, I've photographed more than 400 species and traveled across the U.S.—from northern Maine to Texas, Florida, California, and beyond—always chasing light, moments, and new discoveries.
